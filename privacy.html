Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

Forava Privacy Policy
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how Forava collects, uses, and protects your data when you use our AI-powered cultural gifting platform.

Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Contact information you provide (name, email for account creation)
  • Device contacts you select for gifting (names only, stored locally on your device)
  • Payment information processed through Apple In-App Purchases
  • App preferences and settings (cultural contexts, design choices)

AI-Generated Content:

  • Cultural gift designs you create (stored locally on your device)
  • Text prompts and personalization inputs for AI generation
  • Image preferences and style selections

Usage Information (Optional):

  • App performance data for improvement (only if you enable analytics)
  • Technical error logs for troubleshooting
  • Subscription status and purchase history (through Apple)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Generate personalized cultural gift designs using AI technology
  • Process and deliver digital gifts to your selected contacts
  • Manage your subscription and provide access to premium features
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support when needed
  • Ensure cultural accuracy and appropriateness of generated content
  • Send important service updates and notifications

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes, except in these limited circumstances:

  • AI Services (OpenAI DALL-E 3): Text prompts are sent to OpenAI's API for image generation only. No personal information (names, addresses, contacts) is included in AI prompts. Generated images are returned and stored on your device.
  • Apple Services: Subscription management and payments are processed through Apple's In-App Purchase system. We do not receive or store your payment details.
  • Analytics (Optional): Anonymous usage statistics may be collected if you enable analytics in Settings. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or legal process
  • Safety: To protect our rights or the safety of others

Data Storage and Security

  • Cultural Gift Designs: Stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage
  • Contact Information: Accessed via iOS Contacts framework with your permission; never uploaded to our servers
  • AI Prompts: Temporarily processed for image generation, then immediately discarded
  • Subscription Data: Managed by Apple; we only receive verification tokens
  • All network communications use HTTPS encryption
  • No user accounts or profiles are created on our servers
  • All content stays on your device unless you choose to share it

AI-Generated Content

Important: Cultural gift designs are generated using AI technology (DALL-E 3). While we strive for cultural accuracy and sensitivity, AI-generated content may occasionally contain inaccuracies or unintended representations. We encourage users to review designs before sharing.
  • All AI-generated images are for personal, non-commercial use only
  • You retain the right to use, modify, and share your generated designs
  • We are not responsible for misinterpretations or cultural inaccuracies in AI-generated content
  • You may delete any generated content at any time

Cultural Sensitivity

Forava is committed to respecting diverse cultural traditions:

  • We implement content filters to prevent inappropriate or offensive imagery
  • Cultural designs are based on research and community feedback
  • Users can report culturally insensitive content through the app
  • We continuously update our AI prompts to improve cultural accuracy
  • Feedback from cultural communities is welcomed and valued

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data (all gift designs are on your device)
  • Delete all your data by using the "Delete All Data" option in Settings
  • Export your designs and settings through the app's sharing features
  • Control data collection through Settings (analytics, notifications)
  • Revoke contact access permissions at any time through iOS Settings
  • Request information about data processing by contacting us
  • Cancel your subscription at any time through Apple

Data Retention

  • Cultural Gift Designs: Stored on your device until you delete them manually
  • AI Generation Prompts: Not stored; processed and immediately discarded
  • App Settings: Stored locally until you delete the app
  • Subscription Data: Managed by Apple according to their retention policies
  • Technical Logs: Retained for maximum 30 days if analytics enabled

Subscription Information

For users with active subscriptions:

  • Subscription status is verified through Apple's servers
  • We do not store your payment information
  • All billing is handled by Apple In-App Purchases
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions through iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions
  • Subscription data is retained according to Apple's policies

International Data Transfers

  • AI image generation occurs on OpenAI's servers (may be outside your country)
  • We ensure appropriate safeguards for international transfers
  • Personal information (names, contacts) is never included in international transfers
  • All data transfers use encryption and secure protocols

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do:

  • We will notify you through the app
  • The "Last Updated" date will be changed
  • Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance
  • Significant changes will require renewed consent

Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices:

  • Email: support@forava.com
  • Response Time: We respond to privacy inquiries within 48 hours
  • In-App Support: Use the "Contact Support" feature in Settings

Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • Apple App Store Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Australian Privacy Principles (APP)
  • Other applicable data protection laws
Privacy Summary: Forava is designed with privacy-first principles. All cultural gift designs are stored locally on your device, no user accounts are required, and we only process the minimal data necessary to generate AI-powered cultural gifts. Your data stays on your device.
